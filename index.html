About

Hi, I'm Tanvi, a PGDM student at IMT Hyderabad specializing in Marketing and Finance, with a passion for combining creativity with analytical thinking.

I enjoy building brands, crafting marketing strategies, designing engaging visuals, and solving business problems through data-driven insights. Whether it's creating impactful campaigns, exploring financial concepts, or working on creative projects, I love finding the balance between numbers and storytelling.

Beyond academics, I actively contribute to campus committees, collaborate on diverse projects, and constantly seek opportunities to learn, create, and grow. I believe that the best ideas are born when strategy meets creativity, and I'm always excited to take on challenges that push me outside my comfort zone.

Outside of work, you'll usually find me designing on Canva, painting, exploring new marketing trends, or cooking something delicious — because nothing makes a foodie happier than creating a great meal from scratch. I also love discovering new places and experiences that spark fresh ideas and inspiration.
